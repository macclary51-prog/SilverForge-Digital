Privacy

Privacy information

This page explains general privacy practices for the SilverForge Digital Solutions website. Application-specific practices may be described separately.

Website contact information

When you choose to contact SilverForge Digital Solutions, you may provide information such as your name, business name, email address and project details.

How information may be used

Information you voluntarily provide may be used to:

  • Respond to your message or support request.
  • Discuss a requested project or service.
  • Prepare project information or a quote.
  • Provide support for an existing project.

Contact form behavior

The contact form on this static website is designed to open the visitor's email application. The website itself does not include a database for storing form submissions unless that functionality is added later.

Application privacy

SilverForge applications may have different features and data practices. Review the privacy information connected to each application's official store listing before use.

Third-party services

The website or applications may link to third-party platforms such as GitHub Pages, Google Play or social media services. Those platforms operate under their own privacy policies.

Policy updates

This privacy information may be updated when website features, applications or business services change. The published page should show the date of its most recent review.
