Privacy Policy

LDG Systems privacy policy

This page explains how LDG Systems apps handle device information and user privacy.

System Monitor Privacy Policy

System Monitor does not collect, store, sell, or share personal user data.

The app displays system information from your device, including battery status, battery temperature, battery voltage, charging state, memory usage, storage information, network information, and device details.

This information is used only to display app features directly on your device. LDG Systems does not send this information to an external server.

System Monitor does not require a user account, does not sell personal data, and does not transfer personal user data to outside parties.

Personal Data Not collected

System Monitor is designed to work without user accounts or personal profile information.

Device Information Used locally

Battery, RAM, storage, network, and device information are displayed inside the app for monitoring features.

Background Monitoring For app features

Background or live monitoring features may use Android permissions so the app can continue showing system information.

Third Parties No selling or sharing

LDG Systems does not sell, rent, or share personal user data with third parties.

Other LDG Systems Projects

Other LDG Systems projects may have their own privacy details depending on the features added. Privacy information will be updated if a project adds features that require additional explanation.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as LDG Systems apps continue to grow. Any future updates will be posted on this page.
