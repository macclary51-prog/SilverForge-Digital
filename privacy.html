System Monitor Privacy Policy

System Monitor does not collect, store, or share personal user data.

Information displayed inside the app, such as battery status, memory usage, storage information, network details, and device information, is processed locally on your device for app functionality.

System Monitor does not require user accounts, does not use third-party analytics, and does not sell or transfer user data to outside parties.

If certain features access device-level information, that access is used only to display system information and app functions directly on your device.

Data Collection No personal data collected

No user accounts, no server-side storage, and no third-party sale of data.

Data Usage Local device functionality only

Battery, RAM, storage, network, and device information are used only to display app features on your device.

Third Parties No data selling or sharing

LDG Systems does not sell, rent, or share personal user data with third parties.
